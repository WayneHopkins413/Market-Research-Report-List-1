<p><p>The educational simulation software market can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based educational simulation software is hosted on remote servers, allowing easy access from various devices and promoting collaboration among users. It often features automatic updates and scalability. In contrast, on-premise educational simulation software is installed locally on individual systems, offering greater control over security and customization. This type may require significant initial investment and maintenance but can provide a tailored experience for specific institutional needs.</p></p>
