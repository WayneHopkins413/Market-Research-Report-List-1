<p><strong>The Passenger Vehicle Air Conditioner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reciprocating Type</li><li>Rotary Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passenger vehicle air conditioner market comprises primarily two types: reciprocating and rotary compressors. Reciprocating types use pistons to compress refrigerant, offering high efficiency and effectiveness in a range of conditions. They are widely used in larger vehicles due to their reliability and performance. On the other hand, rotary compressors utilize vanes or rollers for compression, providing quieter operation and compact design, making them ideal for smaller vehicles. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences and vehicle specifications.</p></p>
