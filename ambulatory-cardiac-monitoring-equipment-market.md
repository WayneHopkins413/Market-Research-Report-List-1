<p><p>Ambulatory cardiac monitoring equipment encompasses various types designed to assess heart activity outside clinical settings. Holter monitors continuously record heart rhythms over 24-48 hours, providing comprehensive data for diagnosis. Event monitors capture heart activity during symptomatic episodes, activated by the patient when needed. Mobile cardiac telemetry offers real-time monitoring and data transmission to healthcare providers, ensuring timely response to abnormalities. Other market types may include wearable devices and implantable loop recorders, expanding the scope of remote cardiac assessment.</p></p>
