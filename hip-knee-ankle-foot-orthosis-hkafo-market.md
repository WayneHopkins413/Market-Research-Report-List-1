<p><p>The Hip Knee Ankle Foot Orthosis (HKAFO) market is segmented into two main types: dynamic orthotics and static orthotics. Dynamic orthotics are designed to facilitate movement and support functional mobility, allowing for a range of motion while assisting in the user's gait. Static orthotics, on the other hand, provide rigid support and positioning, often used to stabilize limbs and reduce movement, benefiting individuals with severe weakness or paralysis. Both types play crucial roles in rehabilitation and mobility enhancement.</p></p>
