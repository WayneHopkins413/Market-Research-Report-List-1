<p><p>The anesthetic vaporizer market features two primary types: plenum vaporizers and draw-over vaporizers. Plenum vaporizers utilize a controlled flow of carrier gas to deliver volatile anesthetics in a precise manner, ensuring consistent dosage during procedures. In contrast, draw-over vaporizers rely on the patient's respiratory effort to draw in anesthetic gases, making them suitable for low-resource settings or portable anesthesia. Both types serve essential roles in providing safe and effective anesthetic delivery in various clinical environments.</p></p>
