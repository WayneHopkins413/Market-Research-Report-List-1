<p><p>The Automotive Carbon Composite Driveshaft Market includes two main types of universal joints: rigid and flexible. Rigid universal joints provide a solid connection between driveshaft sections, ensuring efficient torque transfer without flexibility, ideal for high-performance applications. Meanwhile, flexible universal joints accommodate misalignments and shaft angles, allowing for smoother operation and improved drivability in various vehicle conditions. Both types leverage the lightweight and high-strength properties of carbon composites, enhancing vehicle performance and fuel efficiency while reducing overall weight.</p></p>
