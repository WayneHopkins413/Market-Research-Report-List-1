<p><p>Biomass power generation encompasses various equipment types crucial for converting organic materials into energy. Steam turbines convert steam produced from biomass into mechanical energy, while boilers generate this steam by burning biomass. Generators then transform mechanical energy from the turbines into electricity. Pumps are essential for moving fluids within the system, ensuring efficient operation. Additionally, other equipment types may include emissions control systems, conveyors, and feedstock handling machinery, all contributing to the overall biomass power generation process.</p></p>
