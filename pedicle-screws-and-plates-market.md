Pedicle screws are used in spinal surgeries to provide stability by anchoring to the pedicle of vertebrae, facilitating effective spinal fusion. Both types are crucial for treating spinal deformities, trauma, and degenerative conditions, contributing to improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
