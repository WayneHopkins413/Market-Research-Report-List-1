<p><p>The Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) market is categorized based on the type of comonomer used in production: C4, C6, and C8 LLDPE. C4-LLDPE utilizes butene as the comonomer, offering good flexibility and tensile strength. C6-LLDPE, using hexene, provides enhanced clarity and processing characteristics. C8-LLDPE, derived from octene, features superior elasticity and low temperature performance. Each type serves various applications in packaging, agriculture, and consumer goods, catering to specific performance and environmental requirements.</p></p>
