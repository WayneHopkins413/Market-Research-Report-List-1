<p><p>The High Voltage Direct Current (HVDC) power supply market is segmented into two main types: Positive Polarity and Negative Polarity systems. Positive Polarity HVDC systems transmit power using a positive voltage relative to ground, often utilized for long-distance transmission due to lower losses. Negative Polarity systems, on the other hand, use a negative voltage and can be advantageous in specific applications, including underground or underwater transmissions. Both types enhance grid stability and enable efficient power distribution across vast distances.</p></p>
