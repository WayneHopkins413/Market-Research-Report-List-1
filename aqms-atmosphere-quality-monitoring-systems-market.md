<p><p>Atmospheric Quality Monitoring Systems (AQMS) are essential for measuring and analyzing air quality. The market comprises two primary types: Portable Monitoring Systems and Stationary Monitoring Systems. Portable systems are lightweight, enabling flexible deployment in various locations for real-time data collection, ideal for short-term assessments or specific events. In contrast, Stationary Monitoring Systems are fixed installations that provide continuous air quality data over time, making them suitable for regulatory compliance and long-term environmental monitoring in urban or industrial areas.</p></p>
