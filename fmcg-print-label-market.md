<p><p>The FMCG print label market comprises various types tailored for diverse applications. Wet glued labels utilize adhesive applied during the labeling process, suitable for glass and cardboard. Pressure-sensitive/self-adhesive labels use a sticky backing that adheres without heat or water. Liner-less labels eliminate the backing for eco-friendliness. Multi-part barcode labels feature multiple layers for inventory and tracking. In-mold labels are integrated during product molding for durability. Shrink sleeve labels conform to container shapes, providing complete coverage and vibrant graphics.</p></p>
