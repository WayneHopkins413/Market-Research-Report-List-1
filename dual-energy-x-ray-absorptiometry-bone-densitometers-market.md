<p><p>The Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (DEXA) bone densitometer market features two primary types: axial and peripheral bone densitometers. Axial bone densitometers focus on assessing bone mineral density in central skeletal regions, such as the spine and hip, making them ideal for diagnosing osteoporosis. In contrast, peripheral bone densitometers evaluate bone density in peripheral sites like the wrist and heel, offering a portable, cost-effective option for screening and monitoring bone health in various settings.</p></p>
