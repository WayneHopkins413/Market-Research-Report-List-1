<p><p>The ophthalmology cataract surgery devices market comprises key components such as intraocular lenses (IOLs), phacoemulsification devices, and ophthalmic viscoelastic devices (OVDs). Intraocular lenses are implanted to replace the natural lens removed during surgery, enhancing vision. Phacoemulsification devices use ultrasound waves to break down cataracts for easier extraction. Ophthalmic viscoelastic devices are used to maintain eye pressure and protect ocular tissues during surgery. Together, these devices facilitate efficient cataract procedures and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
