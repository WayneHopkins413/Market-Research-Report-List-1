<p><p>The Subcutaneous Implantable Defibrillator (S-ICD) System is an advanced cardiac device designed to monitor and treat life-threatening arrhythmias without the need for transvenous leads. This device is implanted under the skin, primarily in the left lateral chest area, delivering electrical shocks to restore normal heart rhythm when necessary. Its design minimizes complications associated with traditional implantable cardioverter-defibrillators (ICDs), particularly regarding vascular access and infection.</p><p>Market growth for the S-ICD System is being driven by a rising prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, increasing awareness about the benefits of early arrhythmia detection, and advancements in medical technology.
