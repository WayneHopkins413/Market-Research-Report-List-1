<p><p>The recyclable thermosets market encompasses three primary recycling types: mechanical recycling, energy recycling, and feedstock recycling. Mechanical recycling involves reprocessing thermoset materials into new products without altering their chemical structure. Energy recycling focuses on converting thermosets into energy through incineration, generating heat and power. Feedstock recycling breaks down thermosets into their chemical components, allowing for the production of new raw materials. Each method offers distinct advantages for sustainability and resource efficiency in managing thermoset waste.</p></p>
