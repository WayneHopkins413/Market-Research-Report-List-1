<p><ul><li>Firmware over-the-air (FOTA)</li><li>Software over-the-air (SOTA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Over the Air (OTA) updates market encompasses two primary types: Firmware over-the-air (FOTA) and Software over-the-air (SOTA). FOTA involves updating the firmware of devices, ensuring they operate with the latest features and security enhancements, particularly in embedded systems like IoT devices and smartphones. In contrast, SOTA focuses on updating application software, enabling new functionalities and improving user experience. Both technologies offer seamless updates without requiring physical access to the device, enhancing efficiency and user satisfaction.</p></p>
