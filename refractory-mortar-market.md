<p><p>The refractory mortar market encompasses several types tailored for different applications. Clay refractory mortar is known for its versatility and used in industries like ceramics. High alumina refractory mortar offers high-temperature resistance, ideal for metal and glass furnaces. Silicon refractory mortar, containing silica, is effective in high-thermal shock environments, making it suitable for kilns. Lastly, magnesia refractory mortar, containing magnesium oxide, excels in environments with basic slag, often utilized in steel production and other industrial applications.</p></p>
