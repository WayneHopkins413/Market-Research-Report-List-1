<p><strong>The Smart Electric Bidet Seats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tank Type</li><li>Tank-less Type</li><li>Hybrid Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart electric bidet seats come in three main types: tank type, tank-less type, and hybrid type. Tank type bidets have a built-in water heater that stores a limited amount of heated water, providing consistent warmth. Tank-less type bidets heat water on demand, offering unlimited supply but typically requiring more power. Hybrid types combine both systems, allowing for instant heating while also maintaining a small tank for backup. Each type caters to different user preferences and bathroom setups, enhancing comfort and hygiene.</p></p>
