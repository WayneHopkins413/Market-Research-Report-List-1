<p><p>The venous catheter market is categorized into several types based on lumen configuration. Single-lumen catheters are designed for simple intravenous access, allowing for the administration of fluids or medications. Double-lumen catheters provide two separate channels, enabling simultaneous administration of different therapies or the ability to draw blood. Triple-lumen catheters feature three lumens for more complex treatment regimens, often used in critical care settings. Other types include specialized catheters for specific medical applications, enhancing versatility in venous access.</p></p>
