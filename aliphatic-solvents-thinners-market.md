Mineral spirits, with a lower volatility, are commonly utilized for thinning oil-based paints and cleaning applications. Hexane, primarily known for its strong solvent properties, is used in industrial processes and extraction methods. Each type serves distinct applications in industries like automotive, construction, and manufacturing, catering to diverse formulation needs.</p></p>
