<p><strong>The Automotive Exhaust Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Dispersive Infrared</li><li>Flame Ionization Detector</li><li>Chemiluminescence Analyser</li><li>Constant Volume Sampler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive exhaust analyzer market comprises several key technologies. Non-Dispersive Infrared (NDIR) analyzers measure gas concentrations using infrared light absorption, ideal for detecting carbon dioxide and hydrocarbons. Flame Ionization Detectors (FID) quantify organic compounds through ionization in a flame. Chemiluminescence analyzers are used for measuring nitrogen oxides by detecting light emitted during chemical reactions. Constant Volume Samplers collect exhaust samples at a fixed volume, ensuring accurate measurements of pollutants. Each type serves distinct purposes in exhaust emissions testing and compliance monitoring.</p></p>
