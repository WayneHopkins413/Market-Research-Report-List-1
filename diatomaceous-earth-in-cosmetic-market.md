<p><p>Diatomaceous Earth (DE) in the cosmetic market is categorized by color variations—white, yellow-white, and off-white—each reflecting its purity and mineral content. White DE, known for its high silica content, is often used in skincare and makeup for oil absorption and exfoliation. Yellow-white DE contains more iron and is generally less refined, suitable for body scrubs. Off-white DE, being less pure, may include impurities but is cost-effective for bulk formulations in body treatments and environmental applications.</p></p>
