<p><p>The cardiac surgical devices market encompasses several key types. Beating heart surgery systems enable cardiac procedures without stopping the heart, enhancing recovery. Cardiopulmonary bypass equipment temporarily takes over heart and lung function during surgery, allowing complex operations to proceed safely. Cardiac ablation devices target arrhythmias by destroying problematic heart tissue using energy. Additionally, perfusion disposables are single-use items associated with heart-lung machines, ensuring sterile conditions and efficient blood flow management during cardiac interventions. Together, these devices are crucial for successful cardiac surgeries.</p></p>
