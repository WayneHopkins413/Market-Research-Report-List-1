<p><strong>The Embedded Vision Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The embedded vision camera market comprises two primary types: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture images in a two-dimensional format, making them ideal for applications requiring panoramic views or capturing static scenes. In contrast, line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, making them suitable for high-speed applications like conveyor belt inspections or scanning long objects. Both types play vital roles in industrial automation, robotics, and quality control, enabling efficient visual analysis.</p></p>
