<p><p>Vision Guided Robot (VGR) Systems are classified into two main types: 2D VGR Systems and 3D VGR Systems. 2D VGR Systems utilize cameras to interpret flat visual data, enabling tasks like parts recognition and sorting in simpler environments. In contrast, 3D VGR Systems employ advanced depth sensors and imaging techniques to perceive three-dimensional environments, allowing for more complex manipulation and navigation tasks. Both types enhance automation efficiency in industries such as manufacturing, logistics, and assembly.</p></p>
