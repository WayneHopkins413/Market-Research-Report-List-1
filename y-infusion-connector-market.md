<p><p>The Y infusion connector market is segmented into three main types: those with a hemostatic valve, without a hemostatic valve, and others. Connectors with hemostatic valves are designed to minimize blood loss during infusion by preventing backflow. In contrast, connectors without a hemostatic valve lack this feature, allowing for more straightforward application but with higher potential for blood exposure. The 'others' category includes specialized connectors that may incorporate unique designs or functionalities tailored for specific medical scenarios or requirements.</p></p>
