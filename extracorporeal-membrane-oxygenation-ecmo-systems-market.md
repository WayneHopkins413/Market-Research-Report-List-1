<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO) Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>VA (Veno-Arterial) ECMO</li><li>VV (Veno-Venous) ECMO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ECMO systems market is primarily segmented into two types: Veno-Arterial (VA) ECMO and Veno-Venous (VV) ECMO. VA ECMO is used for patients with severe cardiac and respiratory failure, providing both cardiac support and oxygenation by connecting venous blood to an arterial circuit. In contrast, VV ECMO is specifically designed for respiratory support, facilitating gas exchange in patients with respiratory failure while maintaining cardiac function. Both types are crucial in critical care settings, enhancing patient survival rates.</p></p>
