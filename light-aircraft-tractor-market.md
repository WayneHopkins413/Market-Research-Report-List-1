<p><p>The Light Aircraft Tractor market is primarily divided into two types: towbarless and with towbar. Towbarless tractors feature a direct connection to the aircraft, allowing for more maneuverability and reducing the risk of damage to the aircraft's structure. In contrast, tractors with towbars use a detachable connecting device, providing versatility in towing various aircraft types, albeit with some limitations in mobility. Both types are essential for efficient aircraft handling and ground operations in aviation settings.</p></p>
