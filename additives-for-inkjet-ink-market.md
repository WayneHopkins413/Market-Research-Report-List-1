<p><p>The inkjet ink additives market comprises several key types that enhance ink performance. Dispersing agents ensure even pigment distribution, preventing clumping. Leveling agents promote smooth ink application on substrates. Defoamers reduce air bubble formation, which can affect print quality. Emulsifiers facilitate the mixing of oils and water in ink formulations, enhancing stability. Corrosion inhibitors protect printing equipment from damage caused by ink components. Other additives may include stabilizers and adhesion promoters, broadening the functionality and efficiency of inkjet inks.</p></p>
