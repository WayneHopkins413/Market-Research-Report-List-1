<p><p>The loose fill polystyrene packaging market comprises several types, each serving distinct needs. Ordinary polystyrene is basic and cost-effective but lacks rigidity. Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) offers superior insulation and cushioning properties, making it ideal for fragile items. High-Impact Polystyrene (HIPS) combines strength with lightweight characteristics, suitable for both packaging and consumer goods. Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene (SPS) provides enhanced flexibility and durability, often used in environments requiring superior cushioning and impact resistance, such as electronics and delicate merchandise packaging.</p></p>
