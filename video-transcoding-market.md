<p><strong>The Video Transcoding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>H264</li><li>H265</li><li>VP9</li><li>AV1</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The video transcoding market includes various codecs, each serving different needs. H.264 remains popular for its balance of quality and file size, ideal for streaming. H.265 (HEVC) offers improved compression, supporting 4K content but requires more processing power. VP9, developed by Google, is open-source and excels in web streaming. AV1 is a newer, royalty-free codec designed for efficiency and high-quality video at lower bitrates, gaining traction in the industry but facing slower adoption due to encoding demands.</p></p>
