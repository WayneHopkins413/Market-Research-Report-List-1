<p><ul><li>Press Molding</li><li>Casting Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Quartz Floor Market is primarily segmented into two types: Press Molding and Casting Molding. Press Molding involves applying high pressure to quartz materials to shape them into durable floor tiles, ensuring a dense and sturdy finish. In contrast, Casting Molding utilizes liquid quartz mixtures poured into molds, allowing for more intricate designs and patterns. Both methods contribute to the versatility and aesthetic appeal of quartz flooring, catering to a variety of architectural and interior design needs.</p></p>
