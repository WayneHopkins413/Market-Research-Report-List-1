<p><p>The data center rack market comprises three primary types: Open Frame Racks, Rack Enclosures, and Wall-Mount Racks. Open Frame Racks provide accessible and cost-effective storage for network equipment, facilitating airflow and cooling. Rack Enclosures offer security and enhanced thermal management for sensitive equipment, featuring closed designs that protect hardware from dust and unauthorized access. Wall-Mount Racks are ideal for smaller spaces, allowing the mounting of equipment directly on walls, maximizing space efficiency while maintaining accessibility.</p></p>
